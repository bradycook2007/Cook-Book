Salt Flavored Ice Cream

Ice cream is normally a rich, thick treat. What if it was the opposite?.

Ingredients

  • 10 tablespoons of salt
  • 5 cups of water
  • 10 Cups of ice

”The Process”

  1. Mix everything into a blender
  2. Blend until it reaches a ice cream consistency
  3. sprinkle some salt on top
  4. Enjoy
